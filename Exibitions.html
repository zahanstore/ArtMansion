Current & Past Exhibitions

"Visions in Color" - Solo Exhibition

Modern Art Gallery, New York | June 2024

This groundbreaking solo exhibition featured 25 original works exploring the emotional resonance of color. The show received critical acclaim for its innovative use of texture and light.

"Contemporary Perspectives" - Group Show

International Art Fair, London | March 2024

Participated alongside 12 international artists in this prestigious group show. Featured three large-scale abstract works from the "Chromatic Fields" series.

"Elements" - Solo Exhibition

Galerie du Monde, Paris | October 2023

A celebration of natural forms, this exhibition showcased works inspired by earth, water, air, and fire. Included interactive installations alongside traditional canvas works.
