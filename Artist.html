About the Artist

Born in 1996 in Bombay, Zayed discovered a passion for painting at an early age. After graduating from the Arts with honors, they developed a unique style that blends classical techniques with contemporary themes.

Over the past 15 years, Zayed has exhibited in galleries across Europe and North America, with works held in private collections worldwide. Their art explores the relationship between color, emotion, and human perception.

When not in the studio, Zayed teaches masterclasses and mentors emerging artists through the ArtMansion Foundation.

Artist Statement

"My work is an ongoing dialogue between tradition and innovation. I draw inspiration from the natural world, transforming organic forms into abstract compositions that invite viewers to explore their own emotional responses."

"Each piece begins with a moment of connection - with a landscape, an emotion, or a musical phrase. Through layers of color and texture, I aim to capture not just what I see, but how it makes me feel."

"The creative process is both meditative and exhilarating. I work intuitively, allowing each painting to evolve organically until it reaches its own resolution."
